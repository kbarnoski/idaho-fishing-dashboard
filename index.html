Guide Notes

  • DO NOT WADE: At 13,900 cfs this river is dangerous. This is strictly a float fishery right now. Book a drift boat or raft trip.
  • Float access points: Palisades Dam → Irwin → Conant → Cottonwood → Heise. Elevated flows extend take-out times — scout shuttles in advance.
  • Best window is 11 AM–3 PM — cold water temps keep fish slow in mornings. Wait for daily warming to trigger activity.
  • Search and destroy: Cover water to find soft seams; don't pound open flat banks. Fish congregate tight in protected pockets at high flows.
  • By June 5: Flows could drop to 6,000–9,000 cfs if snowmelt moderates. PMDs and Salmonflies may be hitting — that's a world-class window. Monitor the Palisades reservoir release schedule daily as your trip approaches.
