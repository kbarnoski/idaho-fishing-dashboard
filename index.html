3:00–6:00Wind & the slow hours — nymph through it
Push the bite where you can. If the gusts are up, this is nymph time — Pat's Rubber Legs #8–10 (golden-stone nymph) with a Pheasant Tail / PMD nymph #16 dropper through deeper runs and Box Canyon (~1,250 cfs). Fish till you can't see the fly.

Wind kills the dry bite — and today's gusts (~28 mph) are the limiter: fish sheltered water, nymph through the gusts, and save the dry game for the calmer evening.
